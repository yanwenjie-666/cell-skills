# cell-response

> Reviewer Response Letters for Cell Press Journals | Cell Press 审稿人回复信撰写技能

## 概述 | Overview

本技能用于撰写符合 Cell Press 期刊规范和学术礼仪的审稿人回复信（Response to Reviewers / Rebuttal Letter）。Cell Press 期刊的审稿流程和编辑期望与 Nature/Science 有所不同，本技能针对这些差异优化回复策略和行文风格。

---

## 适用场景 | When to Use

- 收到 Cell Press 期刊的 revision request
- 需要逐条回复 reviewer comments
- 需要撰写给 editor 的 cover letter（revision 版）
- 需要制定 revision plan 和策略
- 需要为 appeal letter（被拒后申诉）提供帮助

---

## Cell Press 审稿特点 | Cell Press Review Characteristics

### 编辑角色
- Cell Press 编辑是 **full-time professional editors**（非学术兼职）
- 编辑对论文是否适合期刊有很大决定权
- 编辑可能会提出独立于 reviewer 的意见
- "Revise" 在 Cell Press 通常比 Nature 更积极的信号

### 审稿流程
- 通常 2-3 位 reviewer
- 可能有 cross-reviewer discussion
- 编辑可能要求 additional experiments
- Cell 主刊的 revision timeline 通常为 3 个月

### 回复格式期望
- 对编辑和 reviewer 分别回复
- 逐条编号对应
- 明确标注修改内容在稿件中的位置
- 新增数据/实验用新图编号或补充图

## 工作流程 | Workflow

### Step 1: 分析审稿意见

输入：reviewer comments / editor decision letter

分析维度：
- **每条意见的类型**：
  - Major concern (需要新实验/分析)
  - Minor concern (需要澄清/修改文字)
  - Suggestion (可选择性采纳)
  - Factual error (reviewer 理解有误)
  - Philosophical disagreement (学术观点分歧)

- **意见优先级**：
  - 🔴 必须解决（影响接收决定）
  - 🟡 应该解决（强化论文）
  - 🟢 可选择性解决（锦上添花）

- **策略建议**：
  - 哪些需要新实验
  - 哪些可以通过分析/讨论解决
  - 哪些可以礼貌推回

### Step 2: 制定回复策略

为每条意见制定：
- 回复类型：Agree & Do / Partially Agree / Respectfully Disagree
- 所需行动：新实验 / 新分析 / 文字修改 / 补充讨论
- 预计新增内容：新图 / 补充图 / 新段落

### Step 3: 撰写回复信

#### 信件结构

```markdown
## Response to the Editor

Dear Dr. [Editor Name],

Thank you for the opportunity to revise our manuscript entitled
"[Title]" (Cell-D-24-XXXXX). We are grateful for the constructive
feedback from the reviewers and the editorial team.

We have carefully addressed all comments and have made the following
major changes:
1. [Major revision 1]
2. [Major revision 2]
3. [Major revision 3]

Below, we provide point-by-point responses to each reviewer's comments.
Reviewer comments are in **bold**, and our responses follow in regular text.
All changes in the revised manuscript are highlighted in [red/blue].

---

## Response to Reviewer 1

**Reviewer 1, Comment 1:**
**[Original reviewer comment verbatim]**

[Response]

We thank the reviewer for this insightful comment. [Response content].

We have now [action taken]. This is shown in [new Figure X / revised
Figure X / Supplemental Figure SX]. The relevant text has been revised
on page X, lines XX-XX:

> "[Quoted revised text from manuscript]"

---

**Reviewer 1, Comment 2:**
**[Original reviewer comment verbatim]**

[Response]

...

---

## Response to Reviewer 2

[Same structure]

---

## Response to Reviewer 3

[Same structure]
```

### Step 4: 撰写修改版 Cover Letter

```markdown
Dear Dr. [Editor Name],

We are pleased to submit our revised manuscript entitled "[Title]"
for consideration at [Journal Name].

We have carefully addressed all the points raised by the reviewers.
The major changes include:

1. [Summary of major change 1]
2. [Summary of major change 2]
3. [Summary of major change 3]

We believe these revisions have substantially strengthened the manuscript
and hope it is now suitable for publication in [Journal Name].

Sincerely,
[Lead Contact Name]
On behalf of all authors
```

### Step 5: 质量审查

检查：
- [ ] 所有 reviewer comments 都已回复
- [ ] 回复编号与 reviewer 编号匹配
- [ ] 新增图/数据正确引用
- [ ] 稿件修改位置准确标注
- [ ] 语气专业得体
- [ ] 无攻击性或防御性语言
- [ ] Cover letter 包含修改摘要
- [ ] 使用美式英语

## 回复写作原则 | Response Writing Principles

### 语气与态度

**DO:**
- "We thank the reviewer for this thoughtful comment"
- "We appreciate the reviewer raising this point"
- "The reviewer makes an excellent point that..."
- "We agree with the reviewer that..."
- "We have now addressed this by..."

**DON'T:**
- "The reviewer is wrong because..."
- "This is clearly stated in the paper..."
- "We disagree" (without elaboration)
- "This was already in the supplemental" (too dismissive)
- 任何暗示 reviewer 没认真读论文的语言

### 同意并执行 (Agree & Do)

```
We thank the reviewer for this suggestion. We agree that [explanation
of why this is valuable]. We have now performed [new experiment/analysis],
which demonstrates [result]. This is shown in the new Figure X/Figure SX.
The text has been updated accordingly (page X, lines XX-XX).
```

### 部分同意 (Partially Agree)

```
We appreciate the reviewer raising this point. While we agree that
[concession], we note that [nuance/limitation of suggested approach].
To address the reviewer's concern, we have [compromise action]. We hope
this adequately addresses the concern while maintaining [reason for not
fully complying].
```

### 礼貌推回 (Respectfully Disagree)

```
We thank the reviewer for this thought-provoking comment. We have
carefully considered this suggestion. However, we respectfully maintain
our original approach because: (1) [evidence/reasoning 1]; (2)
[evidence/reasoning 2]; (3) [published precedent if applicable].

To further clarify this point for readers, we have added the following
text to the Discussion (page X, lines XX-XX):

> "[New text addressing the concern]"
```

### 超出范围的要求 (Beyond Scope)

```
We appreciate the reviewer's interest in [topic]. However, we believe
that this analysis/experiment is beyond the scope of the current study
because [reason]. We have added a brief discussion of this as a future
direction in the revised manuscript (page X, lines XX-XX). We hope the
reviewer finds this acceptable.
```

## Cell Press 特定注意事项 | Cell Press-Specific Notes

### 1. Editor 的独立意见
- Cell Press 编辑可能在 decision letter 中有独立评论
- 必须单独回复 editor 的意见（不只是 reviewer 的）
- Editor 意见权重最高

### 2. Cross-Referee Discussion
- 如果 reviewers 之间有分歧，利用这一点
- 可以引用支持你的 reviewer 的意见

### 3. "Essential Revisions" vs "Other Points"
- Cell Press 编辑常将意见分为 Essential 和 Other
- Essential 必须全部解决
- Other 可以更灵活处理

### 4. 字数和篇幅
- 回复信不要太长（reviewer 也会觉得疲劳）
- 每条回复精炼有力
- 长篇解释放在稿件正文/方法/补充材料中

### 5. 引用格式
- 在回复信中引用新加的文献时使用完整格式
- 引用自己稿件用页码/行号
- 引用新图用 "new Figure X" 或 "revised Figure X"

## Appeal Letter 模板 | Appeal Template

被拒后的 appeal（谨慎使用）：

```markdown
Dear Dr. [Editor Name],

We write to respectfully appeal the decision on our manuscript
"[Title]" (Cell-D-24-XXXXX).

We believe the reviewer concerns can be fully addressed because:

1. [Key point 1 — typically pointing out factual errors in review]
2. [Key point 2 — new available data]
3. [Key point 3 — why the work is appropriate for this journal]

We would be grateful for the opportunity to submit a revised version
addressing these points. We are prepared to [specific action plan].

Sincerely,
[Lead Contact]
```

## 输出格式 | Output Format

输出包含：
1. **审稿意见分析表** — 每条意见的类型、优先级、策略
2. **完整回复信** — 逐条 point-by-point response
3. **修改版 Cover Letter** — 修改摘要
4. **修改行动清单** — 需要在稿件中做的所有修改

## 质量标准 | Quality Standards

- 所有 reviewer 意见 100% 覆盖
- 语气始终专业、感恩、合作
- 修改位置准确引用（页/行/图号）
- 新增内容清晰标注
- 无防御性或攻击性语言
- Cover letter 简洁（<500 词）
- 回复信条理清晰，使用编号系统
- 美式英语
